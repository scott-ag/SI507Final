If you would like, you can also explore more state to state comparisons by replacing values in the URL, plugging your choices like such: 'comparestates/(State)/(State)/(Y Variable). The applicable variables can be found below.

Variable Explanation
WhitePct The percent of the State's population that the Census has designated 'White.'
BlackPct The percent of the State's population that the Census has designated as 'Black,' or Black mixed with other races.
Diploma The percent of state residents 25 years and older who have a high school diploma or better.
Income The median household income for all state residents.
Variable Explanation
